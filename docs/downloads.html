NFTs: Secure Downloads

How do I download the audio associated with my NFT copyright?

When you load the song's page on WrapDapp, assuming the publisher has uploaded an mp3 audio file for the song, click on "Download Audio". You will be prompted to sign into your wallet (for example, Metamask) and prove ownership over the song via a digital cryptographic signature. Once confirmed, the audio will be downloaded to your computer.

Who can download an NFT's audio?

The current owner of the copyright NFT associated with the song can download the song's audio. Publishers have the option to make this download available to present and past owners as well.

What format is my audio download delivered in?

All audio files are streamed and downloaded as mp3s.

What can I do with my downloaded audio file?

Whatever you want! As the copyright NFT owner, the digital audio for the song is essentially owned by you. Put it on an audio device and listen to it on the go!
